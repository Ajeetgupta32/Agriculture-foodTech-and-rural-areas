Real Agricultural Problems

Climate Change

Unpredictable weather patterns, extreme temperatures, and irregular rainfall affecting crop yields and farming schedules.

-15% yield reduction ₹50B annual losses

Pest & Disease Management

Increasing resistance to pesticides, new invasive species, and crop diseases threatening food production.

20-40% crop loss ₹220B global impact

Water Scarcity

Limited water resources, inefficient irrigation systems, and drought conditions affecting agricultural productivity.

70% water usage 2.8B people affected

Soil Degradation

Loss of soil fertility, erosion, and contamination reducing agricultural land productivity.

33% soil degraded ₹400B annual cost
